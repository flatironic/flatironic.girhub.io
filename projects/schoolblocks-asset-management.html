SchoolBlocks Asset Management with Gulp

The SchoolBlocks application uses a large number of JavaScript resources, the performance hit from loading dozens of individual files was significant.

By utilizing Gulp to manage project assets, I was able to combine and uglify the JavaScript and SASS files as needed for different hosting environments. Gulp was used to manage every asset including font files, images, SASS and JavaScript.

Google Chrome profiling was used to prove a significant reduction of load/paint time while improving the development process.
