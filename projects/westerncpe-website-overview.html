Western CPE Website Overview

Researching themes and building PhotoShop/InVision prototypes based on a preferred UI kit, we quickly nailed down a new design and general style guide. A general information architecture was designed with the CMO while building UI components using Bootstrap, SASS, jQuery and Handlebars templates as needed. I built the infrastructure to maintain the site almost entirely by content editors.

I picked Umbraco as the CMS to work within our .NET solution. Umbraco development focused on SEO designed pages and a heavy emphasis on usability.
