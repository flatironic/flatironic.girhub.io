SchoolBlocks Sponsorship Concepts

SchoolBlocks is a CMS for schools. The sponsorship system is an idea intended to provide additional school funding with a percentage going to SchoolBlocks.
Introduction: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RucSiyMP-nY

The specifications and design for the sponsorship program needed to be defined prior to starting development. Examples of some of the details that needed to be defined included:
  • The process of signing sponsors up
  • The management of sponsors by the school district and SchoolBlocks
  • Sponsors management of their sponsorships
  • The process of approval
  • Sponsorship visibility on school websites
  • Sponsor editing of their web presence

Using PhotoShop to create concepts and InVision to produce a prototype of the application, I was able to refine and define the design and business approach through collaboration and refinement.

The InVision prototype for the sponsorship system: https://invis.io/847ILW7CZ.
